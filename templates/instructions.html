TO PLAY Tic-Tac-Toe:

  1. Select the Tic-Tac-Toe Button on the homescreen
  2. Use the mouse to click the location on the board you wish to place a marker (the player to move first is randomly decided)
  3. A loading circle will appear when the bot is thinking, followed by a move by the bot. Do not click while the bot is making a move
  4. Try to get three in row
  5. Once the game is complete, click restart to rematch or back to return to the home screen
  6. For more information on the rules of the game, visit Tic-Tac-Toe

TO PLAY Connect4:

  1. Select the Connect4 Button on the homescreen
  2. Use the mouse to click anywhere on the column of the board you wish to place a marker (the player to move first is randomly decided)
  3. A loading circle will appear when the bot is thinking, followed by a move by the bot. Do not click while the bot is making a move
  4. Try to get four in row
  5. Once the game is complete, click restart to rematch or back to return to the home screen
  6. For more information on the rules of the game, visit Connect4
